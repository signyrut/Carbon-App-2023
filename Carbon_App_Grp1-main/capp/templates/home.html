Developers

Our team of students of economics has this semester taken the class of Green Digitalization and App Development. The teacher Mario Blázquez de Paz has been teaching the students to develop and deploy their own app in groups. The programming languages the students have learned is HTML, CSS, Flask, Python and Chart.js. The students have learn how to deploy the app with GitHub, Heroku and Amazon web Service. If you have any questions about the app or the developers, you are welcome to ask questions to the group or contact the members on their LinkedIn profiles.
